# Code of Conduct

Crucible follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1, available at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## TL;DR

- Treat people with respect.
- Disagree about technical decisions on the merits, not on the people
  proposing them.
- Don't post things in our spaces that you wouldn't want pasted into a
  professional Slack channel at work.
- If you see someone being mistreated in this project's spaces (issues,
  PRs, discussions, the Gradio Space, anywhere), tell the maintainers.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates the Covenant, contact
the maintainer listed in the repo profile, or open a GitHub Security
Advisory if you'd prefer the report stay private even from other
maintainers.

Reports are taken seriously. We'll acknowledge within 72 hours and follow
up with action when warranted.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues and PRs, the Gradio
Space, any Discord or Slack we may set up later, and at any in-person or
virtual events where someone is representing Crucible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may, at their discretion, edit or remove comments and code,
ban contributors temporarily or permanently, or take other actions to
maintain a productive working environment. Decisions are explained where
possible and not always negotiable.
